Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our services

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, AI, PSD, and CDR files. For best quality, we recommend PDF files at 300 DPI resolution.

How long does it take to complete an order?

Printing services: 1-3 business days
Website development: 1-4 weeks
IT support: Same day or next business day
Delivery time may vary based on your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (Orange Money, Afrimoney), bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment can be made through WhatsApp or at our store location.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery within Freetown and surrounding areas for Le 25,000. You can also pick up your order at our store for free.

Can I track my order status?

Yes! After placing your order, you'll receive WhatsApp updates on your order progress. You can also contact us anytime to check your order status.

What are your business hours?

Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Sunday: Closed
WhatsApp support available 24/7 for urgent requests.

Do you provide design services?

Absolutely! We offer logo design, graphic design, business card design, and complete branding packages. Our team can create custom designs based on your requirements.

What is included in your IT support services?

Our IT services include computer repair, software installation, virus removal, data recovery, network setup, and IT consulting. We handle both hardware and software issues.

Can I get a quote before placing an order?

Yes! Contact us via WhatsApp with your requirements, and we'll provide a detailed quote. For complex projects, we offer free consultations to understand your needs.
